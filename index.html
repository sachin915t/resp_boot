This is as in Aitareya Upanishad with its प्रज्ञानम् ब्रह्म , prajñānam brahma, or Mandukya Upanishad’s अयम् आत्मा ब्रह्म, ayam ātmā brahma, or “This Self (ātman) is brahman”.
